How to play

Get ready for a fun and educational adventure into the world of wildlife! In this game, you'll learn fascinating facts about animals from around the world.

  • You'll be given some key details about an animal.
  • Based on these clues, you must guess the correct animal.
  • Then, select 6 traits that best describe it.
