How to Play

Welcome to Egg Catcher! Here's how to play:

  • Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the pan left and right
  • Choose from three awesome game modes:
  • Reflex Mode: Be super quick to catch falling eggs!
  • Math Mode: Solve cute little math puzzles by catching the egg with the right answer!
  • Word Mode: Catch letters to build a fun word!
  • Earn points for every egg you catch
  • Avoid missing eggs to keep your score high
  • Have lots of fun and beat your high score!
